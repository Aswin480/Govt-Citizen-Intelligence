NATIONAL POLICY FOR FARMERS, 2007 (Revised 2024 Draft)

1. PREAMBLE
Agriculture is the backbone of the Indian economy. To ensure the economic viability of farming, this policy framework focuses on income security rather than just food security.

2. KEY INITIATIVES
(a) Tech Subsidies: The government provides 50% subsidy on precision farming equipment including Drones and IoT sensors for soil health monitoring.
(b) Minimum Support Price (MSP): MSP will be guaranteed at 1.5 times the comprehensive cost of production (C2).
(c) Crop Insurance: The PM Fasal Bima Yojana covers all non-preventable natural risks from pre-sowing to post-harvest.

3. DIGITAL AGRICULTURE MISSION
A digital ecosystem for agriculture will be created to enable farmers to access real-time market data, weather forecasts, and advisory services. All farmers will be issued a Unique Farmer ID (UFID).
