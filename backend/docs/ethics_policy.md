# Ethics & Transparency Policy

## Purpose
This system is designed to improve public understanding of parliamentary debates,
policies, and government schemes using neutral and explainable AI techniques.

## Data Sources
- Official parliamentary debate transcripts
- Publicly available policy documents
- Government-published scheme information

## Data Usage
- No Aadhaar numbers or personal identifiers are collected
- User inputs are minimal and voluntary
- Data is used only to generate informational insights

## Explainability
- Sentiment and policy impact decisions are rule-based or explainable
- No black-box decisions affect eligibility outcomes

## Limitations
- Eligibility and policy impact results are indicative only
- Final verification must be done through official government portals

## Bias & Neutrality
- The system does not promote political opinions
- Visuals and summaries are non-emotional and neutral
